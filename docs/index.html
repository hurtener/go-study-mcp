go-study-mcp

Turn your notes into study audio

An MCP server that transforms study material into narrated podcasts, deep study guides, flashcards, and speech — with expressive TTS that actually sounds good.

Quick install
curl -sL https://github.com/hurtener/go-study-mcp/releases/latest/download/go-study-mcp-macos-arm64 -o go-study-mcp && chmod +x go-study-mcp

Four tools, one server

🎙️

generate_podcast

Transform study material into a narrated podcast with flexible tone, persona, and language support.

casual · academic · custom
📖

generate_study_guide

Deep expert-level narrated study guide with expressive voice tags for genuine tonal variation.

undergraduate → PhD
🃏

generate_flashcards

Q&A flashcards with timed pauses for active recall. The audio waits for you to think.

basic · intermediate · advanced
🔊

synthesize_speech

Direct text-to-speech with [PAUSE:N] markers for custom pacing and emphasis.

multi-language

Voice that teaches, not just reads

The study guide tool uses expressive audio tags that Gemini TTS interprets for genuine emotion.

[warm] [thoughtful] [curious] [emphasizing] [serious] [normal voice] [pause]
[warm] Bienvenido a esta guía. Hoy vamos a hablar de algo que llevás encima las 24 horas del día.

[thoughtful] Antes de hablar de la piel en sí, necesitamos tener claro el panorama general.

[curious] ¿Y por qué primero la innata y después la adaptativa? Porque la innata es veloz.

[emphasizing] La adaptativa deja células de memoria. Esto es lo más importante de toda la parte.
